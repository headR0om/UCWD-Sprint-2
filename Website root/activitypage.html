Activities

ELEAGUE Major;

Exciting news! The next Counter Strike Global Offensive tournament will be held in Atlanta on January 22-29, 2017. Hosted by ELEAGUE. This will be streamed live on both Twitch and TBS while having its own venue at The Fox Theatre, tickets are currently on sale!

The ELEAGUE Major will feature 8 Challengers competing against the top 8 teams from the previous major(Legends). Challengers will be decided from a LAN qualifier which is yet to be decided. Prize pool will consist of $1,000,000 as well as receiving the title of ‘CS:GO Major Champion’.

Intel Extreme Masters;

On November 19-20 There will be a tournament hosted by Intel in which 12 teams will be battling it out over the $300,000 prize pool. It will be streamed on Twitch while being based in Oakland USA.

Esports Championship Series Season 2 – Finals;

The Esports Championship Series Season 2 is finally coming to an end with; 4 European teams and, 4 North American Teams battling it out over the $750,000 prize pool. This is based in the beautiful city of Anaheim and will be on December 9-11.

Dreamhack Summer 2017;

This is one of the smaller tournaments with a small $100,000 prize pool. 8 teams will battle this out in Jönköping Sweden. The dates will be on June 17-19.
