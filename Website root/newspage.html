EPICENTER 2016;

In the most recent CS:GO tournament Team Dignitas took it home beating Virtus.Pro 2-1 in the finals. They took home the prize of $250,000 while Virtus.pro taking home $100,000. In third and fourth was SK Gaming and Natus Vincere(NaVi). The Tournament was in the city of Moscow Russia and ended October 23rd.

ESL One: New York 2016;

October 2nd Ended a very easy tournament for Na’Vi with them beating SK Gaming 16-6 and taking home that $125,000 grand prize. This was an 8 Team tournament in which Na’Vi won all 3 of the games needed with a 24 round difference in their favour beating SK Gaming by 17 rounds. In Second place was Virtus.pro taking home $50,000.

ESL One: Cologne 2016;

The most recent Major in which 16 Teams battled it out for the share of $1,000,000. It was a very easy final for SK Gaming beating Team Liquid on both maps to win with their only map lost on the semifinals but still pulling through. The date ended was July 10th while starting on July 5th. This was the last Major in which Betting was still around so people won and lost a lot of money in this, due to them ‘making the most of it’.

ELEAGUE Season 1;

This is the largest pot to date with a whopping $1,400,000. The lucky team of Virtus.pro came away with it in the City of Atlanta, with Virtus.pro beating Fnatic 2-0 against all odds and against everyone’s thoughts they still managed to pull through and beat Fnatic while being the underdog.
